It is also important to consider the quality of the materials used in the mattress, as well as its overall construction. Ukizzzdream mattresses are designed with superior construction and high-quality components, ensuring they withstand the test of time.

Mattress types

  • spring

    They feature an open coil spring unit which is one continuous piece of wire formed in the shape of a single spring unit. They are cheap and lightweight but offer the most limited of support in a mattress.

  • pocketed coil

    These coils are unique because each individual spring is wrapped in a fabric “pocket,” which helps the the coil absorb pressure without needing to distribute it to the surrounding springs. As a result, the pocket coil mattress absorbs motion better than other types of spring construction.

  • memory foam

    Invented by NASA in the 1970s to make aircraft cushions safer and then adopted for use in hospital beds, memory foam has an advanced capacity for absorbing pressure and motion. This makes it a popular option for those with certain types of body pain.

  • eco

    Made of Organic Latex Foam, Organic Cotton and Organic Wool. We carefully crafted this natural latex mattress to produce a simple but high-quality mattress that is comfortable, durable and safe.

How to sleep better

The right mattress for you will contain enough supportive layers to hold your spine in correct alignment, regardless of your body weight and sleep position. A lack of support and comfort will make falling asleep in the first place difficult as you struggle to find a sleeping position that actually feels comfortable. Investing in a new mattress means that you’ll have a bed that’s contouring to your body, cradling pressure points and providing plenty of support where it’s needed. All this is going to make it easier and quicker to fall asleep at night.

Allergens like dust mites and pollen can have adverse effects on our sleep, so you may want to consider a mattress that will help keep these allergens at bay. Some mattresses have hypoallergenic properties - latex mattresses achieve this naturally, while other materials can be infused with antimicrobial technology.

As old mattresses sag or collapse, you’ll find that more heat will get trapped in the layers, making it difficult to regulate your temperature, and eventually leading to overheating. New mattresses will help you to regulate your temperature through the night, leading to better sleep.

Improve your comfort

To get the most out of your pressure relieving mattress, it’s important to use a supportive pillow. This will help keep your spine aligned and prevent neck pain.
